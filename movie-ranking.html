The Best Movies According to Rizwana

My Top 3 Movies of all-time.

Forensic

Forensic is a gripping Malayalam thriller that delves into the world of forensic science and crime investigation. The film follows a forensic expert who teams up with a police officer to solve a series of mysterious murders. With its intense plot, well-developed characters, and suspenseful twists, Forensic keeps viewers on the edge of their seats from start to finish.

Ratsasan

Ratsasan is a powerful and thought-provoking Tamil film that explores themes of justice, morality, and the consequences of one's actions. The movie follows a young man who becomes entangled in a web of corruption and revenge, leading to a dramatic climax that challenges the viewer's perception of right and wrong.

Drishyam

Drishyam is a captivating Malayalam thriller that tells the story of a man who goes to great lengths to protect his family from a crime they did not commit. The film is known for its clever plot twists, strong performances, and emotional depth, making it a must-watch for fans of suspenseful storytelling.

These movies have left a lasting impression on me due to their compelling narratives, strong characters, and thought-provoking themes. I highly recommend them to anyone looking for engaging and memorable cinematic experiences.
